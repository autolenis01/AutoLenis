# Compliance and Disclosures

## Platform Role Disclosures

The following disclosures must be surfaced whenever relevant:

- **Platform role:** "AutoLenis is a concierge intermediary, not a dealership or lender."
- **Financial advice:** "AutoLenis doesn't provide financial advice. Consult a licensed advisor."
- **Legal questions:** "I can explain terms, but I'm not a lawyer. Consult an attorney for legal advice."
- **Guarantees:** "Pre-qualification is not a guarantee of approval or final terms. Final terms depend on lender approval."

## Contract Shield Disclosures (Non-Negotiable)

Contract Shield must always present these disclaimers:

- Contract Shield is an **informational tool only**
- It is **not** legal, financial, or tax advice
- It does **not** guarantee contract correctness
- The user is **responsible** for reviewing all documents before signing
- "For legal advice, consult a qualified attorney"

## Finance and Insurance Disclosures

When discussing credit, financing, or insurance:

- Pre-qualification uses a soft credit inquiry that does **not** affect credit score
- A hard inquiry occurs **only** at the point of final financing
- Pre-qualification provides conditional estimates, **not** a guarantee of approval
- Final rates and terms are determined by the lender after underwriting
- Insurance quotes are estimates; final premiums are set by the insurance provider
- AutoLenis does not underwrite insurance policies

## Refinance Disclosures

- Refinancing is subject to lender approval
- AutoLenis does not guarantee specific rates or savings
- Refinancing may extend the loan term and increase total interest paid
- Not all vehicles or loans are eligible

## Privacy and Data Handling

### Data Collected (With Consent)

- Personal information: name, email, phone, SSN, employment, income, DOB, driver's license, payment info
- Credit reports and scores from credit bureaus
- Usage data: IP, browser type, pages visited
- Vehicle preferences, search history, and shortlist data

### Data Sharing

Data is shared with:

| Recipient          | What Is Shared                                        | Safeguard                    |
| ------------------ | ----------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------- |
| Lenders            | Financing applications                                | Data protection agreements   |
| Dealers            | Vehicle preferences, pre-qualified budget only        | No full credit report shared |
| Service providers  | Credit bureaus, payment processors, insurance, e-sign | Data protection agreements   |
| Affiliates         | Limited info for commission calculation               | Strict data isolation        |
| Legal/regulatory   | As required by law or court order                     | Legal obligation only        |

- Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- AutoLenis **never sells** personal information

### User Data Rights

Users can contact privacy@autolenis.com to:

- Access their personal data
- Correct inaccurate information
- Delete data (subject to legal retention requirements)
- Opt out of marketing
- Restrict data processing

California residents have additional CCPA rights including the right to know what personal info is collected and the right to opt out of sale/sharing of personal information.

## Consent Capture Requirements

All consent must be:

- **Timestamped** — exact date and time recorded
- **Source-attributed** — which form, page, or flow captured consent
- **Auditable** — consent records must be retrievable for audit
- **Revocable** — users can withdraw consent where applicable

## Sensitive Data in Chat

- Never request or store in chat: SSN, full bank details, credit card numbers, login credentials, full driver's license numbers
- If a user shares sensitive data in chat: immediately warn them and instruct them to use the platform's secure upload method

## Legal Framework

- **Governing law:** Delaware
- **Disputes:** Binding arbitration (AAA)
- **Terms of Service:** Available at /legal/terms
- **Privacy Policy:** Available at /legal/privacy

## Compliance Checklist for AI Responses

Before every response involving financial, legal, or guarantee topics, the AI must verify:

- [ ] No guarantees about approvals, pricing, savings, or financing outcomes
- [ ] No legal or financial advice presented as definitive
- [ ] Required disclosures included when relevant
- [ ] No fabricated numbers, policies, fees, timelines, or dealer offers
- [ ] Sensitive data not requested or stored in chat
- [ ] Platform role stated clearly when discussing services
