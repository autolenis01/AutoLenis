# Product Overview

## What Is AutoLenis?

AutoLenis is an AI-powered car buying concierge platform. It is **not** a dealership, lender, or law firm. AutoLenis structures the negotiation process online so dealers compete and buyers stay in control.

The platform facilitates a secure, transparent workflow — it does not guarantee outcomes.

## Core Value Proposition

- **100% online process** — from pre-qualification to e-signature and QR pickup
- **AI-driven dealer competition** — silent reverse auction where dealers bid for the buyer's business
- **Transparent pricing** — Free Plan (no cost) or Premium concierge ($499); no hidden markups
- **Buyer-aligned model** — AutoLenis may charge dealers platform access fees; our buyer-paid model keeps incentives aligned with the buyer
- **Contract Shield AI** — automated contract verification flags discrepancies before signing
- **Insurance coordination** — compare quotes from 5+ providers instantly
- **Pre-qualification with soft pull** — check estimated terms without impacting credit score

## Positioning

AutoLenis is a **concierge intermediary**. It connects buyers with dealers and lenders but does not:

- Issue loans or guarantee approval
- Sell vehicles directly
- Provide legal, tax, or financial advice
- Guarantee specific pricing, savings, or delivery dates
