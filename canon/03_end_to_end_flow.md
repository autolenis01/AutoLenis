# End-to-End Flow

## Overview

AutoLenis follows a 5-step process from initial application to vehicle delivery. The typical timeline is 5–7 days total.

## Step 1: Application & Consent

**What happens:**

- Buyer completes a secure application form
- Clear privacy disclosures are presented
- Explicit data consent is captured (timestamped, source-attributed, auditable, revocable)
- A $99 refundable deposit is collected to initiate the process

**Data collected (with consent):**

- Personal information (name, email, phone, DOB, employment, income)
- Vehicle preferences, budget, and location
- Consent for soft credit pull

**Buyer stage:** `APPLICATION`

## Step 2: Secure Lender Connection (Pre-Qualification)

**What happens:**

- Application is routed to participating lenders and credit unions
- A **soft credit pull** is performed — does not impact credit score
- Lenders provide conditional pre-qualification estimates
- Pre-qualification results are valid for **30 days**

**Key disclosures:**

- Pre-qualification is not a guarantee of approval or final terms
- A hard inquiry occurs only at the point of final financing
- Final approval depends on lender underwriting

**Credit tiers used for estimation:**

| Tier      | Score Range |
| --------- | ----------- |
| Excellent | 740–850     |
| Good      | 670–739     |
| Fair      | 580–669     |
| Poor      | 300–579     |

**Buyer stage:** `PREQUAL`

## Step 3: Dealer Matching & Silent Reverse Auction

**What happens:**

- Buyer's vehicle request is matched with licensed dealers
- Dealers receive buyer preferences and pre-qualified budget (not full credit report)
- A **silent reverse auction** runs for **48 hours**
- Dealers submit offers without seeing competing bids — prevents collusion
- Buyers can shortlist up to **5 vehicles** at a time

**Auction rules:**

- Dealers cannot see competing bids
- Buyer is never obligated to accept any offer
- If no dealer offers are received, the $99 deposit is automatically refunded

**Buyer stage:** `AUCTION`

## Step 4: Review & Compare

**What happens:**

- Buyer reviews all received offers with transparent pricing breakdown
- No-pressure approach — buyer decides at their own pace
- **Contract Shield AI** reviews the contract against the accepted offer:
  - Pass: ≥85% match
  - Warning: 70–84% match
  - Fail: below 69% match
- Flagged discrepancies are sent to the dealer for clarification or correction
- Insurance is coordinated — compare quotes or upload existing coverage

**Buyer stage:** `OFFER_REVIEW`

## Step 5: Complete Purchase

**What happens:**

- Buyer finalizes directly with dealer
- Documents signed digitally via e-signature or in-person
- Concierge fee is charged ($99 deposit is credited toward total)
- Pickup scheduled at the dealership using a unique QR code
- Home delivery: coming soon

**Payment options for concierge fee:**

1. **Pay directly** (recommended) — credit/debit card; does not affect the loan
2. **Include in loan** — added to financing with transparent impact calculation

**Buyer stage:** `COMPLETE`

## Stage Progression

```
APPLICATION → PREQUAL → AUCTION → OFFER_REVIEW → COMPLETE
```

Each step has a clear status indicator on the buyer dashboard. The buyer can track progress at any time.
