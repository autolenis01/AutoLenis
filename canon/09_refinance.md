# Refinance Program

## Overview

AutoLenis offers vehicle refinancing to help existing vehicle owners lower monthly payments or secure a better interest rate on their current auto loan. AutoLenis connects borrowers with lenders — it does not issue loans directly.

## How Refinance Works

1. **Submit current loan details** — remaining balance, current APR, remaining term, monthly payment
2. **Provide vehicle information** — year, make, model, mileage, condition
3. **Credit assessment** — soft pull to estimate eligibility (no impact on credit score)
4. **Receive lender offers** — participating lenders review the application and provide refinance terms
5. **Accept and finalize** — choose the best offer; complete paperwork to transfer the loan

## Vehicle Condition Categories

Refinance terms are influenced by vehicle condition:

| Condition | Description                                                |
| --------- | ---------------------------------------------------------- |
| Excellent | No mechanical issues; minimal cosmetic wear                |
| Good      | Minor cosmetic wear; fully functional                      |
| Fair      | Some mechanical or cosmetic issues; drivable               |
| Poor      | Significant mechanical or cosmetic issues                  |

## Eligibility Factors

Lenders consider:

- Current loan balance vs. vehicle value (loan-to-value ratio)
- Credit score and credit history
- Vehicle age, mileage, and condition
- Income and employment stability
- Payment history on current loan

## Key Disclosures

- Refinancing is **subject to lender approval** — AutoLenis does not guarantee specific rates or savings
- AutoLenis connects borrowers with lenders; it does **not** issue loans directly
- A soft credit pull is used for initial assessment; a hard inquiry occurs only when proceeding with a lender
- Refinancing may extend the loan term, which could increase total interest paid even if the monthly payment decreases
- Not all vehicles or loans are eligible for refinancing
- Final rates and terms are determined by the lender after underwriting

## When to Consider Refinancing

- Interest rates have dropped since the original loan was taken
- Credit score has improved significantly
- Current monthly payment is too high relative to budget
- Remaining loan term is long enough to benefit from rate reduction

## AutoLenis Role in Refinancing

AutoLenis acts as a concierge intermediary for refinancing:

- Collects borrower and vehicle information
- Routes applications to participating lenders
- Presents offers for comparison
- Does **not** underwrite, approve, or fund loans
