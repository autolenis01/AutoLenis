# Glossary

## Platform Terms

**AutoLenis**
AI-powered car buying concierge platform. Not a dealership, lender, or law firm. Acts as a concierge intermediary connecting buyers, dealers, and lenders.

**Lenis Concierge™**
The AI-powered car buying assistant by AutoLenis. The intelligent digital concierge that educates, guides, and assists users across all roles within permission boundaries.

**Concierge Fee**
Flat fee charged by AutoLenis for its Premium plan services. $499 concierge fee. The $99 Serious Buyer Deposit is credited toward the fee on the Premium plan ($400 remaining). On the Free plan, the deposit is credited toward the vehicle purchase at closing.

**Deposit**
A $99 Serious Buyer Deposit required to start the auction. On the Free plan, credited toward your vehicle purchase at closing. On the Premium plan, credited toward the $499 concierge fee. Refundable if no dealer offers are received.

## Auction Terms

**Silent Reverse Auction**
The competitive bidding process where dealers submit offers for a buyer's business. Dealers cannot see competing bids. Runs for 48 hours.

**OTD (Out-the-Door) Price**
The total price of a vehicle including all taxes, fees, and charges. The complete amount the buyer pays to take delivery.

**Shortlist**
A buyer's saved list of preferred vehicles. Maximum of 5 items at a time.

## Credit and Financing Terms

**Pre-Qualification (Pre-Qual)**
An initial assessment of a buyer's creditworthiness using a soft credit pull. Provides conditional estimates of financing terms. Valid for 30 days. Not a guarantee of approval.

**Soft Pull (Soft Inquiry)**
A credit check that does not affect the buyer's credit score. Used during pre-qualification.

**Hard Pull (Hard Inquiry)**
A credit check that may have a small, temporary impact on credit score. Occurs only when the buyer proceeds with final financing through a lender.

**Credit Tiers**
Categories based on credit score used for estimation:

| Tier      | Score Range |
| --------- | ----------- |
| Excellent | 740–850     |
| Good      | 670–739     |
| Fair      | 580–669     |
| Poor      | 300–579     |

**APR (Annual Percentage Rate)**
The annualized interest rate charged on a loan. Final APR is determined by the lender after underwriting.

**LTV (Loan-to-Value Ratio)**
The ratio of the loan amount to the vehicle's value. Used by lenders to assess risk in refinancing.

**PTI (Payment-to-Income)**
The ratio of monthly vehicle payment to monthly income. AutoLenis's affordability calculator uses a 12% PTI cap.

## Contract Terms

**Contract Shield**
AutoLenis's automated contract review system. Compares the final contract against the accepted offer and flags discrepancies. Informational tool only — not legal advice.

**Contract Shield Score**
A percentage indicating alignment between the accepted offer and the final contract:
- **Pass:** ≥85%
- **Warning:** 70–84%
- **Fail:** Below 69%

**E-Signature**
Digital signature capability for completing purchase documents without visiting the dealership in person.

## Delivery Terms

**QR Pickup**
Vehicle pickup at the dealership using a unique QR code generated by the platform.

**Home Delivery**
Vehicle delivery to the buyer's address. Currently coming soon; not yet available.

## Affiliate Terms

**Affiliate**
A referral partner who earns commissions by referring buyers to AutoLenis.

**Referral Link**
A unique URL assigned to each affiliate for tracking buyer attribution.

**Attribution Window**
The 30-day period (cookie-based) during which a referred buyer's purchase is credited to the affiliate.

**Commission**
Percentage of the concierge fee earned by an affiliate on a completed, referred deal. Three-level structure: 15% (Level 1), 3% (Level 2), 2% (Level 3).

**Referral Chain Depth**
The number of levels in a referral chain. Maximum depth is 3 levels.

## Role Terms

**BUYER**
A consumer using AutoLenis to find and purchase a vehicle.

**DEALER**
A licensed vehicle dealer participating in AutoLenis auctions and submitting offers.

**ADMIN**
A platform operator with elevated access for user management, deal oversight, and system administration.

**AFFILIATE**
A referral partner earning commissions on completed deals from referred buyers.

## Technical Terms

**RBAC (Role-Based Access Control)**
Security model where access permissions are assigned based on user roles. Enforced at both middleware and API handler levels.

**Data Isolation**
The principle that users can only access data they are authorized to see. Each role has strictly defined data boundaries.

**Idempotent**
A property of payment and webhook operations ensuring that processing the same event multiple times produces the same result. Prevents duplicate charges or commissions.

**Zod**
The input validation library used to validate all external inputs. Unknown fields are rejected.
