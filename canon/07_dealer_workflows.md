# Dealer Workflows

## Overview

Dealers participate in AutoLenis through a dedicated dashboard. They receive matched buyer requests, submit competitive offers via silent reverse auction, and coordinate document submission and vehicle pickup.

## Dealer Onboarding

1. Dealer submits application through the platform
2. Admin reviews and verifies licensing and compliance
3. Dealer account is activated with dashboard access
4. Dealer profile is configured with inventory specialties and service areas

## Silent Reverse Auction Participation

### How the Auction Works

- Buyer creates a vehicle request with preferences and pre-qualified budget
- Matching dealers receive the request (vehicle preferences and budget only — not full credit report)
- Dealers submit offers with **all fees included** and a complete **out-the-door price**
- Dealers **cannot see competing bids** — this prevents collusion and ensures genuine competition
- The auction runs for **48 hours**
- Buyers review all offers and choose the best deal — they are never obligated to accept

### Submitting an Offer

Dealer offers must include:

- Vehicle details (year, make, model, trim, VIN)
- Base vehicle price
- All dealer fees itemized
- Out-the-door (OTD) price including taxes and fees
- Financing terms if applicable
- Any add-ons or warranties (clearly itemized)

### Offer Rules

- All prices must be accurate and complete
- No bait-and-switch — the final contract must match the submitted offer
- Contract Shield AI will verify alignment between offer and contract
- Discrepancies flagged by Contract Shield must be addressed promptly

## Post-Acceptance Workflow

Once a buyer accepts an offer:

1. Dealer is notified of acceptance
2. Dealer submits the final contract
3. Contract Shield AI reviews the contract against the accepted offer
4. If discrepancies are flagged, dealer must clarify or correct
5. Buyer completes insurance verification
6. E-signature or in-person signing
7. Dealer schedules pickup and generates QR code

## Document Requirements

Dealers must provide:

- Final purchase contract
- Vehicle title documentation
- Disclosure documents as required by state law
- Any warranty or add-on documentation

Documents are uploaded securely through the platform.

## Dealer Dashboard Features

| Feature                | Description                                      |
| ---------------------- | ------------------------------------------------ |
| Active buyer requests  | Matched requests available for bidding            |
| Submitted offers       | Status tracking for all offers                    |
| Accepted deals         | Active deals in progress                          |
| Document uploads       | Secure upload for required deal documents          |
| Pickup scheduling      | Coordinate pickup date and QR code generation      |
| Contract Shield flags  | View and respond to contract discrepancies         |

## Privacy Boundaries

- Dealers receive buyer vehicle preferences and pre-qualified budget only
- Dealers do **not** receive buyer credit reports or full identity details until authorized
- Dealer data is isolated — dealers cannot see other dealers' offers or data
