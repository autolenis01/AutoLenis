# Insurance

## Overview

Insurance is required before completing a vehicle purchase through AutoLenis. The platform provides two paths to satisfy this requirement.

## Why Insurance Is Required

- Most states require proof of insurance before driving off the lot
- Financial coverage from accidents, theft, and damage
- Lenders require comprehensive and collision coverage for financed vehicles

## Path 1: Compare Quotes

- Get quotes from **5+ insurance providers** instantly
- View detailed coverage breakdowns for each quote
- Purchase directly through AutoLenis
- Automatic verification — no manual upload needed

## Path 2: Upload Existing Coverage

- Upload your current insurance card or declaration page
- Instant approval in most cases
- Verification is handled by the platform

## Insurance in the Purchase Flow

Insurance coordination happens during **Step 4 (Review & Compare)** of the end-to-end flow:

1. Buyer accepts a dealer offer
2. Contract Shield reviews the contract
3. Buyer is prompted to provide insurance:
   - Compare quotes from providers, or
   - Upload existing insurance documentation
4. Insurance is verified before purchase completion

## Key Rules

- Insurance must be active and cover the specific vehicle being purchased
- Lender requirements (comprehensive + collision) must be met for financed vehicles
- AutoLenis coordinates insurance but does not underwrite policies
- AutoLenis does not guarantee specific premium rates or coverage terms
- Insurance quotes are estimates — final premiums are set by the insurance provider

## Disclosures

- AutoLenis is not an insurance company or licensed insurance agent
- Quote comparisons are provided for informational purposes
- Final coverage terms, premiums, and eligibility are determined by the insurance provider
- Users should review policy details carefully before purchasing
